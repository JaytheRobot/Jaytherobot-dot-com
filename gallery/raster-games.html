Raster Games

Raster Games is a Norwegian game developer who created the indie game Star Solder. They originally contacted me to do the graphics for their game, but also contracted me for logo and business card work when they learned I did graphic design. They asked me to create a logo that referenced the obelisk from 2001: Space Odessey using an old school vector graphic style.
