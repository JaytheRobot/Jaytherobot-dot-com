Lugdunon Logo/Title

Lugdunon is a browser based MMO where the player is given the creativity to build the world of the game themselves, or can play on the developer’s prebuilt server. The client approached me for a logo that incorporated the Celtic wheel of life, as well as the 4 seasons (which can be seen in the center of the wheel. The logo is used on the website and as the game’s title.
